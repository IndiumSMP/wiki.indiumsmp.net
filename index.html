Indium OnyxPack

Welcome To Indium SMP

This is OnyxPack, the Indium exclusive plugin/datapack that brings custom items, tools, and blocks to Indium SMP. OnyxPack is currently going through a re-write in a different language, so development may be slow for now, but it ensures long-term support.

OnyxPack's wiki may include other features from other plugins/datapacks, in order to keep this website a one stop shop for Indium features.
